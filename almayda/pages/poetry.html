129

Reminiscing the past years, months, weeks
I see myself in a pitch-black empty room
spending solitude with the devils I have known for long
tick tock I hear the clock
still no reason to get up
tick tock I hear the clock
hoping that when I fall asleep
there is no waking up
Just my daily routine

Once, on my normal blue day
Somebody knocked
strange
I peeked through the door,
told the devils to hide,
let you in,
no words,
only silence.

I wake up
something's not right
Two of the devils are gone
Maybe they'll come back later
or maybe never
Either way I'm not hoping for anything
Tick tock I hear the clock
and again
I hear you knock

I get up.
